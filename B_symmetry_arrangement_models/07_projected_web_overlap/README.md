# 07_projected_web_overlap

## Hypothesis
Current lattice is an overlap-core of projected webs rather than a direct shadow of intersecting bodies.

## Why It Is Plausible
Stable line-arrangement overlaps can create structured subwebs that are not traces of geometric body intersections.

## Witness
A pair-of-webs construction whose overlap core reproduces the lattice grammar.

## Kill Criteria
Overlap produces too much extraneous structure and only works after heavy manual trimming.

## Stages
- baseline
- prune
- search
- analyze
- finalize
