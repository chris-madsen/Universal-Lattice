# 10_orbit_selected_subweb

## Hypothesis
Current lattice is a symmetry-motivated subset of line or edge orbits inside a richer web.

## Why It Is Plausible
The lattice looks deliberately selected rather than like the whole available structure.

## Witness
A small union of natural orbits reproduces the lattice without arbitrary line deletion.

## Kill Criteria
No symmetry-based orbit selection can explain the observed pruning.

## Stages
- baseline
- prune
- search
- analyze
- finalize
