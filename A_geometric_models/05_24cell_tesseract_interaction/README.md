# 05_24cell_tesseract_interaction

## Hypothesis
Current lattice arises from interaction between 24-cell and tesseract, or their webs.

## Why It Is Plausible
A tesseract explains axial structure well, while the 24-cell explains rich diagonal families.

## Witness
A natural union/intersection/decomposition where vertical and diagonal families come from distinct 4D sources.

## Kill Criteria
