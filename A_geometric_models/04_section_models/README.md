# 04_section_models

## Hypothesis
Current lattice comes from a section of the 24-cell rather than from a projected 1-skeleton.

## Why It Is Plausible
Some arrangements are more naturally explained by section geometry than by direct edge projection.

## Witness
A section graph or section-induced arrangement whose 2D shadow matches the lattice grammar.

## Kill Criteria
Section-derived node classes or fans fundamentally differ from the target lattice.

## Stages
- baseline
- prune
- search
- analyze
- finalize
