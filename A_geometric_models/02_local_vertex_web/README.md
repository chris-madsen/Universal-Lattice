# 02_local_vertex_web

## Hypothesis
Current lattice is a local vertex-neighborhood web from the 24-cell rather than a whole projected web.

## Why It Is Plausible
The lattice looks like a local scaffold with a strong central node, fans, and three main verticals.

## Witness
A local star or small orbit neighborhood whose induced arrangement matches the lattice grammar.

## Kill Criteria
Local fan combinatorics and node signatures cannot be aligned even after soft normalization.

## Stages
- baseline
- prune
- search
- analyze
- finalize
